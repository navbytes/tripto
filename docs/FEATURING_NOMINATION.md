## What's the story (the pitch — lead with uniqueness)

Tripto is the shared trip organizer built for the whole group — not the solo
business traveler. One itinerary the entire party sees: every flight, stay, and
plan on a single timeline, each shown in **its own local time**. The people who
matter but don't want another app — grandparents, an occasional companion — open
a **read-only web link**, no account, no install. It's the only trip planner that
spans "the group chat to grandma."
